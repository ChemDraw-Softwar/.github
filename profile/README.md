# ChemDraw – Smart Chemical Structure Drawing & Productivity Tool

**ChemDraw** is a powerful, flexible chemical structure drawing solution designed to help chemists, researchers, and educators create publication‑ready structures, reaction schemes, and laboratory documents.  
With **ChemDraw**, you can transform complex molecular ideas into clean, professional diagrams.

## About ChemDraw

**ChemDraw** combines the simplicity of a drawing canvas with the power of a cheminformatics engine. Unlike basic drawing tools, **ChemDraw** offers deep customization: bond angles, stereochemistry, templates, and reaction arrows.  
**ChemDraw** works seamlessly with your existing electronic lab notebook (ELN) and provides real‑time chemical property calculations.  
Whether you design drug candidates or teach organic chemistry, **ChemDraw** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**ChemDraw** respects your research privacy — all data stays under your control with optional local storage.  
With **ChemDraw**, you gain clarity, reduce drawing errors, and produce publication‑quality figures faster.

## Key Features of ChemDraw

- **Structure Drawing** – **ChemDraw** automatically snaps bonds at correct angles.  
- **Template Library** – **ChemDraw** includes amino acids, nucleic acids, sugars, and labware.  
- **IUPAC Naming** – Generate systematic names inside **ChemDraw** from drawn structures.  
- **NMR Prediction** – **ChemDraw** estimates 1H and 13C chemical shifts.  
- **Reaction Mechanisms** – Draw curved arrows, electron flow, and transition states in **ChemDraw**.  
- **Spectral Analysis** – Predict IR, MS, and UV‑vis spectra using **ChemDraw**.  
- **Property Calculator** – Compute logP, molecular weight, TPSA, and H‑bond donors in **ChemDraw**.  
- **Batch Processing** – Analyze multiple structures at once with **ChemDraw** batch mode.  
- **Templates** – Save reaction schemes inside **ChemDraw** for reuse.  
- **Export Formats** – Export your **ChemDraw** data to CDX, CDXML, MOL, SDF, SMILES, or PNG.  
- **Cross‑Platform** – Use **ChemDraw** on iOS, Android, and web.  
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Navigate **ChemDraw** faster with hotkeys for bonds, rings, and arrows.  
- **Push Notifications** – Get alerts from **ChemDraw** when batch calculations complete.  
- **Database Search** – Find structures inside **ChemDraw** using substructure or similarity search.  
- **Undo/Redo** – Step back through any drawing action inside **ChemDraw**.
